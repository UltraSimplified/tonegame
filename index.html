tonegame

TL;DR: Click "Play clue" to play one or more notes. Move the note(s) to the empty box and press "Test" to see if you win.

Click the notes at the top to play them. Each click costs one point.

Click "Play all" to play all the notes in sequence. This costs four points.

Drag the notes you think are being played in the clue to the empty box.

Each incorrect click on "Test" costs two points. If you are correct you win ten points and a new clue is generated.

The level increases with your score. Each level brings more notes on the board or in the clue.
